How to play

Each day we take one real question we've polled the British public on — and you guess what percentage said it.

You have 5 guesses to land within 2 points of the real figure. After each guess you'll learn whether the answer is higher or lower, and how close you are:

🎯 Within 2 You win
Hot Within 5
Warm Within 10
Cool Within 20
Cold More than 20 away

The window narrows with every guess. So do your options. New question daily at midnight, London time.

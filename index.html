How to play

Each day we take one real question the British public have been polled on, and you guess what they said.

You have one guess. Use your instinct to estimate what the public think. How good is your crowdsense?

🎯 On the pulse Within 2
Hot Within 5
Warm Within 15
Cold Within 25
Out of touch More than 25 away

Your score is simply how many percentage points you were off. 0 is perfect, lower is better. Within 2 and you're on the pulse; score within 5 to keep your streak.

New question daily at midnight, GMT.

All figures from real polling of the British public; each day's source is shown with the answer.

Privacy

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the game.

Your guess. When you play, the number you guess is sent to our server and pooled anonymously with everyone else's, so we can show how you compare with the crowd. It isn't linked to your name, your email, or any identifier.

Your stats. Your streak, scores and history are stored only in your own browser, never sent to us. Clearing your browser data resets them.

Email. If you subscribe, we store your address solely to send you the daily question. There's an unsubscribe link in every email.

Analytics. We use privacy-friendly analytics that count visits without cookies and without tracking you across sites.
